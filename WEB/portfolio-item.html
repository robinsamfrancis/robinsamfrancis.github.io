Automatic Pill DispenserProject in progress

Abstract

A lot of people, especially elderly people end up with several pills to take at several different times per day, to stay healthy. But complex medication schedules can lead to mistakes like missing doses, taking incorrect amounts, or taking medicines at the wrong times. Depending on which medications are missed, this can cause serious effects on an older person's health.

So this project is on a machine that automatically dispenses the right pills at the right moment with minimal errors. This makes taking daily medications simple and efficient by preventing under and over dosage of the medicine. One of the major peculiarities of this project is that it notifies the patient about the correct timing of taking the medication through a vibrator placed on the patient's hand. The caretaker can control the machine through an app which will also notify the caretaker whether the patient has taken the medication. The machine is also equipped with an emergency calling system, which can be used by the user in case of any emergency.
